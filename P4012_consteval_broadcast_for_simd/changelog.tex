\section{Changelog}
This paper was originally published as \parencite{P3844R2}, which additionally
contained a re-specification of [simd.math].
\parencite{P3844R4}, which was voted to plenary by LWG, only contains the [simd.math]
changes that make conversion behavior more compatible with the \code{<cmath>}
function overloads.
Since there was strong concern over the \code{consteval} broadcast constructor
change in LWG, this part was split into its own paper, separating the
controversial and uncontroversial parts.

Changes since LEWG saw this paper as~\cite{P3844R1}:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Remove [simd.math] discussion and wording changes.
  \item Add discussion of concerns raised in LWG\@.
  \item Shorten the discussion of the design space, discussing only what was
    accepted by LEWG in Kona.
  \item Move content around to arrive quickly at the change and its consequences.
\end{itemize}
